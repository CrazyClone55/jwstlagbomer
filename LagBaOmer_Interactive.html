Lag BaOmer Today
How the holiday is observed now — and the debates surrounding it.
In Israel
Largest national bonfire night of the year
100,000+ pilgrims at Mount Meron
Concerts, parades & street festivals in every major city
2021 Meron crowd crush (45 killed) sparked new safety laws
In the Diaspora
Chabad-Lubavitch parades in NYC, LA, and worldwide
Synagogue school outdoor bonfires & barbecues
Secular Jews participating culturally through food & outdoor activities
Current Debates
Environmental concerns — cities banning or limiting bonfires
Feminist reframings focused on spiritual light over Rashbi's male-centered legacy
Ongoing cultural vs. religious participation debate in secular Israeli society
